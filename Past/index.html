The Past

The world stretches and snaps back into place. Sunlight fills your room. Your phone reads Today, 3:07 PM.

You remember this moment. Earlier, a message appeared from your friend Mia:

Mia: “Hey, want to review the tough chapters together?”

The first time, you ignored it and scrolled on your phone instead. This time, the same notification appears… waiting.

You: “Yeah, let's study now.”

The afternoon unfolds differently. You and Mia review formulas, quiz each other, and fill the gaps in your notes. The exam still scares you—but your understanding feels stronger. You actually used your time.
