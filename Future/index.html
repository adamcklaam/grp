The Future

Dates and icons rush past your eyes like a tunnel of notifications. Everything slams to a stop.

Your room looks the same, but the light is different. Your phone says: Tomorrow, 10:14 AM.

On your laptop, the grades page is already open. One course is highlighted, waiting for you to click.

You move the cursor. Your result appears on the screen. You pass the exam and got a pretty good grade.

It feels strange. You don't remember the questions. You don't remember answering anything. You just jumped here.

The relief doesn't feel like it belongs to you. It's like watching someone else's life from the outside.
